I'm currently a Ph.D. student at Seoul National University.
Previously, I was an undergraduate intern at HOLI Lab (Graduate School of Data Science, SNU) and SNUVL Lab (Dept. of CSE, SNU). My research focuses on evaluation and agentic systems, including GUI agents for complex, narrative-driven tasks and personalized visual persuasion.
Broadly, I'm interested in the intersection of embodied agents, multimodal reasoning, and social intelligence, aiming to build AI systems that interact naturally and meaningfully with humans. My work focuses on developing embodied conversational agents that combine verbal and nonverbal communication, while also exploring evaluation methodologies and value alignment to ensure these agents act in accordance with human norms and values.
